Ethical Financial Support

Msaada wa Kifedha wa Kimaadili

SokoRada helps informal traders and farmers prepare better loan applications. This is a support tool, not a system that automatically denies you a loan.

SokoRada inasaidia wafanyabiashara na wakulima kuandaa maombi bora ya mkopo. Hii ni zana ya msaada, si mfumo unaokukataa mkopo kiotomatiki.

Your Consent & Privacy Idhini na Faragha Yako

1. We collect only what is needed for financial readiness guidance.

1. Tunakusanya tu yale yanayohitajika kwa mwongozo wa utayari wa kifedha.

2. We never share your data without your selected SACCO choice and explicit request.

2. Hatushiriki data yako kamwe bila chaguo lako la SACCO na ombi lako wazi.

3. We never use gender, tribe, religion, or protected info for scoring.

3. Hatutumii jinsia, kabila, dini, au habari zilizolindwa kwa tathmini.

4. You can withdraw consent and delete your profile completely at any time.

4. Unaweza kuondoa idhini na kufuta wasifu wako kabisa wakati wowote.
